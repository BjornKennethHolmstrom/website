I hope these can enlighten up your day, by both giving a laugh and teaching something new and profound if you read the pages linked to by the bold text!

But.. we [don't need anything](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sa%E1%B9%85kh%C4%81ra#Conditioned_things)!

Shh... don't tell anyone, [I am having fun making this stuff up](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reality)

* * *

— What is the difference between you and I?

— We are both empty

* * *

— What does God do to create the universe?  \[<- notice the present tense \]

— Nothing. God is encompasses subject and object, and so, never does a thing. Lazy bastard!

* * *

— What is the question to which there is no real answer?

— [All questions are that question](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prat%C4%ABtyasamutp%C4%81da). The answers to all questions are equally unreal

* * *

— What is the difference between God and a dog?

— The letters are reversed.

— Then what is the similarity between God and a dog?

— Neither actually exist outside the mind

— Then why are we talking about God and a dog?

— Both point to [nothing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%9A%C5%ABnyat%C4%81)

* * *

— Why do you never finish anything?

— There is no thing that can be finished. And no time in which to finish it

* * *

— Why is it impossible to stop?

— Because you never actually move!

* * *

— Why is the poorest the richest?

— [To have nothing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Detachment_\(philosophy\)) is to be the [source of creation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moksha)

* * *

— Where are you going?

— To work

— What do you do?

— Nothing

— Seriously?

— There is nothing else to do. It is [all that ever happens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impermanence)

* * *

— I just discovered [I am not a human being](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C4%80tman_\(Hinduism\))!

— Then what kind of organism are you?

— An organ of Truth, an ambassador of nothing

* * *

— Is there a spherical [Minecraft](https://www.minecraft.net/)?

— Yes, [you are in it!](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_mysticism)

note: Minecraft is an immensely popular (over 100 million copies sold) sand-boxed based video game partly based on placing 3D-blocks (cube-shaped)
