---
title: "A Heart of Gold Has No Color"
date: 2018-10-23
coverImage: "poetry-header-text.png"
---

```
A heart of gold has no color
yet its brilliance 
is beyond blinding

What I want to tell you
is that I love you 
Instead I just pass by briefly

It is better this way
behind a courteous curtain
you keep your charm
and come to no harm
```
