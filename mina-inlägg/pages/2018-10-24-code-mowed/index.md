### Project Overview

MOWED is a web-based platform that brings the power of mathematical optimization to end-users across various fields. By leveraging browser-based technologies, MOWED enables complex decision-making problems to be solved directly on the user's device, ensuring data privacy and accessibility.

## Collaboration Opportunities

To get involved, please reach out through my [contact page](https://bjornkennethholmstrom.wordpress.com/contact/) or open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/BjornKennethHolmstrom/MOWED).

## Project Goals

- Democratize access to powerful optimization tools

- Push the boundaries of browser-based computational capabilities

- Create a comprehensive platform for tackling real-world optimization challenges
