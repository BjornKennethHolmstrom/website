**Get financing for equipment ($1200/€1200/~12000 SEK)**

- Helmet
- Headwear
- Gloves
- Bodysuit
- Underwear
- Back protection
- Boots

**Get financing for the education and test ($1185/€1185/11850 SEK)**

**Complete practice of driving skills**

 

1. Low-speed (lågfart)
2. High-speed (högfart)
3. Breaking (bromsövningar)
4. City driving (stadskörning)
5. Countryside driving (landsvägskörning)

**Complete practical & theoretical education**

- Risks, part I (180 min + pauses)
- Risks, part II

**Complete theoretical education**

- Test (kunskapsprov)

**For the license at the Swedish Transport Administration**

- Check eye sight and get certificate (synintyg)

[Take a photo](https://www.trafikverket.se/korkort/ovrigt/obligatorisk-fotografering-hos-trafikverket-infor-provet/)

**[Do the test!](https://www.trafikverket.se/korkort/korkortsprov/motorcykel/korprov-for-motorcykel-a1-a2-a/)**

Buy a motorbike and enjoy!
