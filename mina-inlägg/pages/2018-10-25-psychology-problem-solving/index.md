**Dealing with paranoid people or people who have delusional thoughts**

- Avoid arguing
- Avoid affirming the paranoia / delusional thoughts
- Focus on persons feelings, have empathy for emotions
- Ask questions, let the person tell more about their fears: Why do you think that? Do you want to tell me more?
- Help them feel safer and more comfortable
- Help maintain a positive state of mind:
    - Model positive thinking
    - Model optimism
- Help put into perspective
- Encourage healthy balanced habits; food, exercise etc.
- Encourage contribution in areas they excel
- Prepare for crisis
