## Overview

I am working on a diverse range of innovative projects spanning artificial intelligence, community engagement, health, economics, gaming, and data visualization. These projects offer unique opportunities for collaboration across various technical disciplines and domains of expertise.

## Projects and Collaboration Opportunities

**BeSAI (Benevolent Spiritual AI)**

- Goal: Develop an ethical and benevolent AI system

- Seeking: AI researchers, ethicists, philosophers, cognitive scientists

**CommuniTree**

- Purpose: Connect municipalities with residents for volunteer work

- Seeking: Full-stack developers, UX designers, community engagement experts

**DaMP (Diet and Meal Planner)**

- Focus: Optimal meal planning based on nutritional data

- Seeking: Nutritionists, dietitians, algorithm developers

**EPiC (Exercise Programmer in Code)**

- Aim: Comprehensive exercise browsing and program creation

- Seeking: Fitness experts, UX designers, mobile app developers

**ESP (Economic Simulation Project)**

- Objective: Large-scale economic simulations

- Seeking: Economists, data scientists, visualization experts

**HeaRTS**

- Concept: Game promoting peace through music and collaboration

- Seeking: Game developers, musicians, conflict resolution experts

**HuGe VisiON**

- Purpose: Visualize and explore complex information networks

- Seeking: Data visualization experts, network analysts, UX designers

**JSUsCH²R**

- Function: Represent daily activities with emojis

- Seeking: Frontend developers, UX designers

**MOWED (Mathematical Optimization With End-user Devices)**

- Goal: Make advanced optimization accessible via web browsers

- Seeking: Optimization experts, WebAssembly developers, UI designers

**QuInCA (Quantum-Inspired Cognitive Architecture)**

- Aim: Model human cognition using quantum-inspired algorithms

- Seeking: Cognitive scientists, quantum computing experts, AI researchers

**SharedSpheres**

- Aim: Create a collaborative ecosystem uniting humans, AI, (and eventually animals, and extraterrestrial intelligences) to tackle planetary and cosmic challenges.

- Seeking: Systems engineers, AI experts, ecologists, animal behaviorists, cosmologists, and xenobiologists.

**TiTo (Time Tracker)**

- Purpose: User-friendly project time tracking

- Seeking: UX designers, productivity experts, frontend developers

**UST (Universal Search Tree)**

- Objective: Create flexible search trees for complex hierarchical data

- Seeking: Algorithm designers, data structure experts, domain specialists

**Z∞ (Z-Infinity Calendar)**

- Concept: Zoomable circular calendar visualization

- Seeking: SVG experts, UX designers, data visualization specialists

## How to Collaborate

If you're interested in contributing to any of these projects, please either:

- Contact me here: [https://bjornkennethholmstrom.wordpress.com/contact/](https://bjornkennethholmstrom.wordpress.com/contact/)  
    and/or

- Visit the respective GitHub repository of the project you are interested in for more detailed information. You can find them here: [https://github.com/BjornKennethHolmstrom](https://github.com/BjornKennethHolmstrom). Open an issue or pull request with your ideas or contributions

I welcome collaborators from all backgrounds and levels of experience. Whether you're a seasoned professional or an enthusiastic beginner, your unique perspective could be valuable to these projects.

Join me in pushing the boundaries of technology and creating tools that can make a positive impact on society!
