_Advanced P2P Synchronization Technology_

## Project Overview

CosmicSyncCore is the foundational technology, originally aimed at powering the SharedSpheres collaboration platform but later with the extended purpose to support any application. It's designed to provide robust, scalable, and efficient peer-to-peer (P2P) data synchronization across vast distances - even cosmic ones! The vision is to support a non-profit, global platform for apps with close to no server costs, immensely reducing the environmental footprint of digital technology as well as providing a completely secure way for users to protect their personal data and information.
