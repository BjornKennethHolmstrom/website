#### peace

A minimalist web platform focused on ending armed conflicts worldwide through understanding, dialogue, and direct action. Available in multiple languages including English, Arabic, Ukrainian, Russian, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and Hebrew.

**Core Principles:**

- Direct action over movement-building

- Inclusive participation

- Evidence-based understanding

- Practical solutions

[Website](https://bjornkennethholmstrom.github.io/peace/en/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/BjornKennethHolmstrom/peace) | [Discord](https://discord.gg/yQ76qBguMF)

#### faith for peace

A platform for interfaith dialogue and collaboration toward peace, emphasizing common ground between religious and secular perspectives. Available in English, Swedish, Hebrew and Arabic.

[Website](https://bjornkennethholmstrom.github.io/faith-for-peace/en/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/BjornKennethHolmstrom/faith-for-peace) | [Discord](https://discord.gg/ABDUpp7sdN)

#### Unity Beyond the Known (In development)

A website for investigating what could be done by refocusing efforts from conflicts into exploring the inner and outer unknowns. Note that the monetary numbers created by Claude are off and should be corrected.
