Spiralize is an interactive web platform that helps people understand and explore Spiral Dynamics - a powerful framework for understanding human consciousness evolution and value systems development.

## Features

- **Interactive Assessment**: A comprehensive questionnaire to discover your "center of gravity" in the Spiral Dynamics model

- **Visual Exploration**: An interactive spiral diagram showcasing different developmental stages

- **Multilingual Support**: Available in both English and Swedish

- **Detailed Insights**: In-depth explanations of each developmental stage and their characteristics

- **Educational Resources**: Rich content about the origins and applications of Spiral Dynamics

- **Mobile-Friendly**: Responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices

## Technology

Built with modern web technologies:

- Svelte/SvelteKit

- TypeScript

- Tailwind CSS

- D3.js for visualizations

## About

Spiralize is designed to make the principles of Spiral Dynamics accessible and actionable, helping individuals and organizations understand themselves and the world better through this unique developmental framework. The project offers practical tools for personal and collective growth, inspired by the work of Clare W. Graves, Don Beck, and Chris Cowan.
