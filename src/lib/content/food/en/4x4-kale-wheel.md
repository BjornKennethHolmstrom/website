~ _bring fourth a number drill_ ~

**Ingredients**

![](/creations/images/food/bring-fourth-a-number-drill_1.jpeg)

_fresh_

- Avocado

- 4 kale leaves

- Lime

- Olive oil

- Papaya

_dry_

- 16 cashew nuts

- Chia seeds

**Preparation**

1. Take a few leaves of a large kale, preferably four.

3. Make a roll out of the leaves and cut the roll finely.

5. Cut the resulting long strands in half as well.

7. Fry the kale in olive oil until you are satisfied with the consistency.

9. Cut an avocado in four pieces.

11. Cut four slices of a lime.

13. Cut four slices of papaya.

15. Find four cashew nuts, four times.

17. Go four placing everything on a plate as in the picture.

19. Sprinkle chia seeds on top of everything, and you have got yourself a 4x4 Kale Wheel!
