~ _better almond spread than eating someone dead_ ~

**Ingredients**

_fresh_

![](/creations/images/food/better-almond-spread-than-eating-someone-dead_1.jpeg)

- Coral lettuce

- Carrots

- Shiitake mushrooms

- Olive oil

- Water

_dry_

- Almonds

- Salt

- Your favorite bread

**Preparation**

_the spread_

Blend almonds together with some olive oil, water and salt

![](/creations/images/food/better-almond-spread-than-eating-someone-dead_2.jpeg)

_the topping_

1. Clean, peel and cut carrots

3. Clean and cut mushrooms

5. Gently fry mushrooms and carrots in a bit of oil

7. Decorate the plate with coral lettuce

9. Put the spread on the slices of bread

11. Cover the spread with the mushroom and carrot topping

13. Add some sunflower seeds
