_~ can you beet this colorful variation? ~_

I admit that I have placed most of the puffs and seeds one by one in these photos  
If I had hand-placed the chia seeds too I would have advanced to full-time food-monk

**Ingredients**

_fresh_

![](/creations/images/food/can-you-beet-this-colorful-variation_1.jpeg)

- Apple cider vinegar

- Avocado

- Coral lettuce

- Kiwi

- Mandarin

- Olive oil

- Peanuts

- Red beet

- Sweet potato

- Tomato

- Zucchini

_dry_

- Chia seeds

- Gojiberries

- Pumpkin seeds

- Quinoa puffs

- Raisins

- Salt

- Sunflower seeds

**Preparation**

![](/creations/images/food/can-you-beet-this-colorful-variation_2.jpeg)

1. Peel half a sweet potato and slice it

3. Boil the slices for 15 minutes

5. Boil a red beet for 30 minutes

7. Peel and slice the beet

9. Slice a quarter of a tomato, a quarter of  
    a zucchini, a third of an avocado and a kiwi

11. Peel and part a mandarin

13. Arrange everything on a large plate

15. Decorate with peanuts, gojiberries, raisins,  
     pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, millet puffs  
     and chia seeds

17. Add red coral lettuce around the sides

19. Sprinkled some apple vinegar, olive oil and  
     salt as dressing. Enjoy!
