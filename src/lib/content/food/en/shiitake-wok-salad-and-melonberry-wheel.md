**Ingredients**

_fresh_

![](/creations/images/food/a-simple-wok-then-fruits-and-berries-for-dessert_4.jpeg)

- Blueberries

- Cabbage

- Carrot

- Coral lettuce

- Galia melon

- Garlic

- Lemon

- Olive oil

- Mulberries

- Peanuts

- Pineapple

- Shiitake mushrooms

- Spinach

- Soy sauce

_dry_

- Chia seeds

- Dried dates

- Dried gojiberries

- Raisins

- Sunflower seeds

**Preparation**

_first prepare the wok-salad_

1. Heat olive oil in a pan

3. Add a minced garlic clove and a carrot chopped in thin sticks

5. Fry for a few minutes

7. Add sliced shiitake mushrooms and soy sauce

9. Stir fry for another couple of minutes

11. Add finely cut cabbage and spinach

13. Keep on stir frying for a little while and finally squeeze over the juice from half a lemon

15. Lay the red coral lettuce around the borders of a plate, and place the wok in the middle

17. Decorate with chia and sunflower seeds, peanuts and dried dates, raisins and gojiberries

![](/creations/images/food/a-simple-wok-then-fruits-and-berries-for-dessert_2.jpeg)

_to prepare the melon-berry wheel_

1. Cut pineapple and galia melon

3. Place the fruit slices and berries on a plate as it appeals to you

5. Sprinkle over with chia seeds and sunflower seeds

Enjoy!
